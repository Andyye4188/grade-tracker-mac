# Grade Tracker

A macOS desktop app for BISZ (Shenzhen Bay American International School) Grade 7 students to track grades across all subjects.

## 9 Subjects

- Speech & Debate
- English 7
- Chemistry
- East Asian History
- Chinese 7
- Computer Science 7
- Algebra & Geometry I
- Physics 7
- Biology 7 New

## How It Works

Each subject uses the same grading system:

| Type | Weight |
|------|--------|
| Major (tests, exams) | 60% |
| Minor (homework, quizzes) | 40% |

For each entry you add, you record:
- **Score earned** (e.g. 85)
- **Score possible** (e.g. 100)
- **Type** (Major or Minor)

The app calculates your overall percentage per subject and your GPA across all 9 subjects.

## Features

- **Dashboard** — overview of all subjects with your current average
- **Add Score** — log a new score with date, subject, earned/possible points, and type
- **Statistics** — detailed breakdown per subject showing Major/Minor averages and earned/possible points
- **Report** — full table view of all subjects, GPA, strongest and weakest subjects
- **Goals** — set a target percentage per subject and track whether you're on pace
