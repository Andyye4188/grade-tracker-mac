# Grade Tracker

A simple macOS app for BISZ Grade 7 students to track grades across 9 subjects: Speech & Debate, English 7, Chemistry, East Asian History, Chinese 7, Computer Science 7, Algebra & Geometry I, Physics 7, and Biology 7 New.

Each subject uses Major (60%) + Minor (40%) weighting. Add scores with earned/possible points, view your stats, and track your GPA across all subjects.

Download the latest release at [github.com/Andyye4188/grade-tracker-mac/releases](https://github.com/Andyye4188/grade-tracker-mac/releases).
